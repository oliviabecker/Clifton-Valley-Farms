About the Farm

making life a little greener

At Clifton Valley Farms, we grow plants and flowers with care, supplying businesses with high-quality, healthy greenery. From offices to stores, our plants are ready to thrive in any space.

We also love sharing our plants with the local community. Our roadside stand offers seasonal baskets and potted plants, giving everyone a chance to bring a little greenery home.

Quality and consistency are at the heart of everything we do. Every plant is grown responsibly in our greenhouse and prepared to meet the needs of our customers.

What we grow matters - to us, to our partners, and to everyone who takes a piece of it home!
