The Stand

Fresh From the Greenhouse

Stocked daily

Our roadside stand is the heart of our connection with the local community. It’s where we showcase the best of what’s blooming in the greenhouse and the field. We believe that access to beautiful, healthy plants should be easy and spontaneous.

Whether you need a last-minute gift, a refresh for your porch pots, or just a little something to brighten your kitchen table, you’ll find it here. We restock every morning to ensure you get the freshest quality possible.
